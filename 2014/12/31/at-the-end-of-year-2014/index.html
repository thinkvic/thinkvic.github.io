At the End of Year 2014

Dec 31, 2014

2014 is about to end. What happened to me in 2014?

At the beginning of the year, if you ask me what is the most important to me. I would answer art.

At the end of the year, if you ask the same question, I would answer health.

To explain the former, “life is short, art is long.” This is a saying printed in a metal pencil box that I earned as an award at middle school.

To explain the latter, “life is really really short!” This is my words. :) Yes, indeed, I don’t want to end up in a philosophical argument about body and mind. But the simple truth is that without healthy body, anything is zero. A quick calculation shows that I’ve already lived half of the life Steve Jobs lived!

I did a quick summary of all the photos taken this year. The total number is a surprising 1600+. Here I picked 22 out of them.

This subset of 22 tries to cover as many subjects as possible, architecture, art, philosophy, nature, season, animal, music, fashion, design, day, night, indoor, outdoor etc.
