TO THE YEARS IN GRADUATE SCHOOL - POEM

Aug 1, 2013

Marooned,
At maroon and white?
Researched,
In the lab or labyrinth?

Materials and files,
Everything just piles.
Wood, gravel, asphalt,
Nature’s gifts!

To build a house,
Enclosing our dream.
To construct a road,
Connecting to the sky!

Chapel, pond and lake,
Only appearances?
Yellow and green,
Ginkgo’s falling leaves!

Fast, medium, slow,
A rhythmic moving.
Time and space,
Which one is dancing?

Spring, summer, fall,
Spring, summer, fall,
The true, the good,
And the beautiful!

–Written in a midsummer night, August 2013

PS: This poetry is dedicated to the long years at graduate school. I tried to use a poetry to summarize the life and thoughts there.
