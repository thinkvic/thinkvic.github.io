Happy birthday and The History of Programming Languages

Jun 27, 2015

1995 seems a miraculous year! Three languages that are still very popular today were born in 1995. Happy 20th birthday to PHP, JavaScript and Java!

Here is a summary of the popular languages in the past few decades.

1957, FORTRAN:

1972, C: influenced by B (BCPL, CPL), ALGOL 68, Assembly, PL/I, FORTRAN

1983, C++: influenced by C, Simula, ALGOL 68, Ada, CLU, ML

1991, Python: influenced by ABC, ALGOL 68, C, C++, Dylan, Haskell, Icon, Java, Lisp, Modula-3, Perl

1995, PHP: influenced by Perl, C, C++, Java, Tcl

1995, JavaScript: influenced by Lua, Scheme, Self, Java, C, Python, AWK, HyperTalk

1995, Java: influenced by Ada 83, C++, C#, Eiffel, Generic Java, Mesa, Modula-3, Oberon, Objective-C, UCSD Pascal,

 

Software examples:

FORTRAN: SPSS, NASTRAN (FEA software)

C, C++: Adobe series, Autodesk, Microsoft Office

PHP, JS, Java: Web apps
