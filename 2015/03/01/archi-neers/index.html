Archi-neers

Mar 1, 2015

This year’s Pritzker prize went to Frei Otto. He is a pioneer in the field of lightweight structures. Interestingly, it seems that the prize committee starts to pay attention to the architects/engineers who integrates structures and architecture. (if I remember correctly, this might be the 1st time)

http://www.pritzkerprize.com/2015/jury-citation

I built my website and this blog in 2014. One of the major themes was in mind: archi-neering.

I encountered this word by chance then and found it very good to describe what I have been believing since my academic training: the architecture and engineering as an integrate whole.

As the years pass by, I was surprised to find that this was not emphasized both in academia and industry. However, I have been able to find some architects/engineers who practice(d) this philosophy and finished some fantastic works.

So, I use this opportunity to make a summary. In the future, when I have time, I plan to do more research and get into more details.
