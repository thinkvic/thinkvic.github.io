This blog collects and documents my ideas and thoughts on architecture, engineering, philosophy, art, design, etc

Three main categories:

  • archi-neering: the synthesis, macro-micro, design, the nature of technology…
  • phi-art: the true, the good, the beautiful…
  • poetry: the randomness, the stream of conscious…

I have lived in 4 cities in both China and USA. two big and two small. two north and two south. So this blog is also about tales of 4 cities.

The 4 places have interesting characteristics. The altitude ranges from 27N to 40N. The longitude is about 115E and 75W. the population ranges from 20K, 200K, 2M, to 20M.

The most populated is Beijing, where you can see people everywhere. The least populated is the town where I studied in the state of Mississippi, USA, with a population of only 20,000. You cannot see people anywhere. It can be foreseen that after many many years, its population still does not need even an ‘int’ (32-bit data type in computer language) to store.
